We are pleased to announce that a new shipping service with DHL Parcel Direct US and new destination countries with Hongkong Post EEX and DPEX will be available from September 1, 2017. We currently offer a DHL Parcel Direct US shipping service to the United States which has now been renamed as DHL Parcel Direct US Expedited. We are now offering a new DHL Parcel Direct US shipping service to the United States called DHL Parcel Direct US Standard, which is a cheaper and slightly slower service.
